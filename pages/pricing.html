Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans

What happens after my 5-day free trial?
After your 5-day free trial, you'll need to choose a paid plan to continue using BizGenius. You can upgrade at any time during your trial. If you don't upgrade, your access will be limited until you subscribe.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
What's included in the 5-day free trial?
During your 5-day free trial, you get full access to all features of the Pro plan, including unlimited text chat, 5 AI voice calls, advanced business analysis, and Wikipedia research. No credit card required to start.
How does billing work?
We offer both monthly and yearly billing. With yearly billing, you save 20% compared to monthly payments. You'll be billed automatically at the start of each billing period. You can change or cancel your plan anytime.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your chat history and business information are never shared with third parties. We comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations.
What if I need more features?
We're constantly adding new features based on user feedback. If you need something specific, please contact our support team. For enterprise customers, we offer custom AI training and API access.
